## Plan Details

### Free — $0 / forever

Best for trying InsiteChat before committing. Includes 1 chatbot, 50 messages per month, and 30 pages of training content.

### Starter — $15 / mo

For small businesses getting started. Expands to 4,000 messages per month and 1,000 pages.

### Growth — $30 / mo

For growing teams. Adds a second chatbot, 10,000 messages per month, 10,000 pages, 4 team members, weekly auto-sync, and custom branding.

### Scale — $150 / mo

For high-volume businesses. Up to 3 chatbots, 40,000 messages per month, 50,000 pages, 10 team members, auto-sync every 3 days, a custom domain, and API access.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card for the Free plan?">
    No. The Free plan requires no payment information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as a message?">
    Each reply your chatbot sends to a visitor counts as one message.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit my message limit?">
    Your chatbot stops responding when the message limit is reached. Upgrade your plan to restore access immediately.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as a page?">
    A page is one URL crawled from your website or one document section indexed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan at any time?">
    Yes. Upgrades apply immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does auto-sync work?">
    Auto-sync frequency varies by plan. Manual syncing is available on all paid tiers at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I remove the InsiteChat badge?">
    Custom branding on Growth and Scale plans removes the InsiteChat badge from the chat widget.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What can I do with API access?">
    The Scale plan API lets you manage messages, knowledge sources, and retrieve conversations programmatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is annual billing available?">
    Yes. Annual billing and enterprise pricing are available — contact support for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
