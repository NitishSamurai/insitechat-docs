# Website Crawler

The website crawler allows you to train your chatbot by pointing InsiteChat at a website URL. It automatically crawls, extracts, and indexes all accessible pages.

## Add a Website Source

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in to InsiteChat and select the chatbot you want to train.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Sources">
    In the left sidebar, click **Sources**, then click **Add source**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Website">
    Select **Website** as the source type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your URL">
    Enter your website's root URL (e.g., `https://example.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the crawl">
    Click **Crawl**. InsiteChat begins indexing your site automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor progress">
    Watch the progress indicator. The source card changes to **Ready** when indexing is complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Indexed

The crawler captures product pages, blog posts, FAQs, and informational pages. It extracts the main text content from each page.

<Warning>
The crawler does not process images or embedded videos. Only text content is indexed.
</Warning>

## Keeping Content Up to Date

You can re-sync your website content to pick up changes:

- **Manual sync** — Available on all paid plans. Trigger a re-crawl at any time from the Sources page.
- **Automatic sync** — Frequency depends on your plan:
  - **Starter** — Monthly
  - **Growth** — Weekly
  - **Scale** — Every 3 days

## Limitations

<Warning>
Pages behind authentication or blocked by `robots.txt` are not crawled or indexed.
</Warning>
