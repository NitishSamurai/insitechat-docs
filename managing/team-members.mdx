# Team Members

<Warning>
**Coming soon.** Team workspaces, invitations, and role-based access are in active development. The seat counts in your plan are reserved for this feature — your final allocation will match the limits shown below when it ships. Until then, every chatbot is owned by a single account.
</Warning>

You will be able to invite your teammates to your InsiteChat workspace so they can collaborate on your chatbot alongside you. There will be no per-seat charges — team member access is included in your plan.

## Team Member Limits by Plan

| Plan | Team members |
|------|-------------|
| Free | 1 |
| Starter | 1 |
| Growth | 4 |
| Scale | 10 |

## How to Invite a Team Member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Select your chatbot and click the **Settings** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Team section">
    Scroll to the **Team** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email address">
    Type the email address of the person you want to invite into the invite field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click **Invite** to send them an invitation email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They join your workspace">
    Your teammate receives an email with a link to accept the invitation and join your workspace. Once they accept, they have immediate access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Team Members Can Do

All team members in your workspace can:

- Create and edit chatbots
- Manage knowledge sources (add, update, remove, sync)
- View conversations and analytics
- Add and edit custom Q&A pairs
- Configure chatbot settings

## Manage Existing Members

To remove a team member, go to the **Settings** tab, find the member in the Team section, and click **Remove**.

<Note>
Team member limits depend on your plan. If you need to add more collaborators, upgrade your plan from the **Settings** tab.
</Note>

<Tip>
Invite your support team lead so they can monitor conversations and add custom Q&A pairs when they spot wrong or missing answers.
</Tip>
