# Analytics

Track chatbot performance, understand customer inquiries, and leverage data for continuous improvement.

## Key Metrics

The analytics dashboard displays six primary metrics:

| Metric | What it measures |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Total conversations** | Number of chat sessions |
| **Total messages** | Cumulative message exchanges |
| **Most asked questions** | Frequently asked customer topics |
| **Unanswered questions** | Questions your chatbot couldn't answer — knowledge gaps |
| **User satisfaction** | Thumbs up / thumbs down ratings from visitors |
| **Message volume over time** | Usage trends and patterns |

## Access Analytics

Open your dashboard and click the **Analytics** tab in the left sidebar.

## Improve Your Chatbot with Analytics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Analytics">
    Go to your dashboard and click **Analytics** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review top questions">
    See which questions visitors ask most often. Ensure your chatbot handles these well.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check unanswered questions">
    Identify questions your chatbot couldn't answer — these are knowledge gaps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Q&A pairs or update sources">
    Create custom Q&A pairs for unanswered questions, or update your content sources with the missing information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-sync your sources">
    If you updated source content, trigger a re-sync from the Sources page in your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Unanswered questions are the most valuable signal for improving your chatbot. They reveal exactly where your knowledge base has gaps that need attention.
</Tip>

## Message Limits by Plan

| Plan | Messages per month |
|------|--------------------|
| Free | 50 |
| Starter | 4,000 |
| Growth | 10,000 |
| Scale | 40,000 |

When you approach your message limit, consider upgrading via **Settings > Billing**.
