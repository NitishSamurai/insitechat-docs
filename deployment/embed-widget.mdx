# Embed Widget

The InsiteChat embed widget adds a chat icon to the corner of your website. When visitors click it, a full chat interface opens. No plugins or developers required — takes under 5 minutes.

## Add the Widget to Your Site

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in to InsiteChat and select the chatbot you want to deploy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Embed tab">
    Click the **Deploy** or **Embed** tab in your chatbot settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the snippet">
    Copy your unique JavaScript snippet. It includes your chatbot ID and looks like this:

    ```html
    <script src="https://widget.insitechat.ai/chat.js" data-chatbot-id="YOUR_CHATBOT_ID"></script>
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste into your website">
    Add the snippet just before the closing `</body>` tag in your website's HTML.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and publish">
    Save your changes and publish your site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the widget">
    Visit your live site and click the chat icon to verify the chatbot works as expected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Your chatbot is live immediately after the snippet is added. No redeployment, plugin install, or developer is required.
</Note>

## Platform-Specific Instructions

### WordPress

Use a header/footer plugin like **Insert Headers and Footers**, or manually edit your theme's `footer.php` file via **Appearance > Theme Editor**. Paste the snippet just before `</body>`.

### Shopify

Go to **Online Store > Themes > Edit code**. Open your active theme's `layout/theme.liquid` file and paste the snippet before the closing `</body>` tag.

### Wix

Go to **Settings > Custom Code**. Add the snippet and set the placement to **Body - end**.

### Generic HTML / Static Sites

Paste the snippet directly before `</body>` in any HTML file. This works with Squarespace, static site generators, and any other platform that lets you edit HTML.
