# Customization

Change your chatbot's name, colors, logo, and welcome message to match your brand. Custom branding is available on the Growth plan and above.

## What You Can Customize

- **Chatbot name** — displayed in the chat header
- **Welcome message** — the first message visitors see when they open the widget
- **Widget colors** — button and header color to match your brand
- **Avatar / logo** — appears in the chat header and on the button icon
- **Button position** — placement of the chat button on the page
- **"Powered by InsiteChat" badge** — removable on Growth and Scale plans
- **Custom domain** — available on the Scale plan only

## Customize Your Widget

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in to InsiteChat and select the chatbot you want to customize.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Appearance">
    Click the **Appearance** or **Settings** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your settings">
    Update the chatbot name, welcome message, colors, and logo. A real-time preview shows your changes as you make them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save**. Changes take effect immediately on the next page load — no re-embed required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Write a welcome message that feels natural and matches your brand voice. A friendly, specific greeting — like "Hi! Ask me anything about our pricing or features." — performs better than a generic "How can I help you?"
</Tip>
