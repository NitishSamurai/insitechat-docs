# Lead Capture

Collect visitor names, emails, and phone numbers directly inside the chat widget. All leads are stored in your dashboard and exportable as CSV.

Lead capture works around the clock — visitors can submit their contact details even outside business hours.

## What You Can Collect

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Custom fields (define your own labels and input types)

## Set Up Lead Capture

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in to InsiteChat and select the chatbot you want to configure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Lead Capture">
    Click the **Lead Capture** tab in your chatbot settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable lead capture">
    Toggle lead capture on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your fields">
    Select which fields to collect — name, email, phone, or custom fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the trigger">
    Choose when the form appears: **before the chat starts** or **after the first message**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The lead capture form is now active on your widget.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing and Exporting Leads

Open the **Leads** section in your dashboard sidebar to view all captured leads with timestamps. Use the **Export CSV** button to download your leads for use with CRM systems, email marketing tools, or other platforms.

<Tip>
Ask for less up front. Collecting just an email address reduces form abandonment. You can always follow up for additional details later.
</Tip>

<Note>
All leads are stored securely in your InsiteChat dashboard. You can view, filter, and export them at any time.
</Note>
