# Notion

Connect your Notion workspace and transform pages, databases, and wikis into queryable chatbot knowledge — without reformatting your content.

## Connect and Train in Three Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Notion">
    In your InsiteChat dashboard, click **Connect Notion**. Authorize the integration and select the pages or databases you want to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your content">
    Search and select specific pages, databases, or entire workspaces to import.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import and train">
    InsiteChat processes your content, creates embeddings, and your chatbot is immediately ready to answer questions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
InsiteChat only sees pages you explicitly share during the Notion authorization step.
</Note>

## What Gets Extracted

InsiteChat extracts structured elements from your Notion pages, including:

- Text paragraphs
- Headings
- Bulleted and numbered lists
- Tables
- Code blocks
- Toggle blocks
- Callouts

## Keep Your Chatbot Current

When your Notion content changes, re-import and re-sync your pages or databases from the InsiteChat dashboard to keep your chatbot's knowledge base up to date.
