# Shopify

The InsiteChat Shopify app installs as a **Theme App Extension** — you add the InsiteChat block through your theme editor and the AI chatbot appears in the bottom corner of every storefront page (with auto-hide on cart and checkout, on by default).

The widget on a Shopify store is the same widget every other InsiteChat customer ships. Color, position, language, greeting, lead-capture fields, and every other behavior are configured from your InsiteChat dashboard at insitechat.ai — Shopify just hosts the script tag for you.

<Note>
The public Shopify App Store listing is currently in review. Until it appears in the App Store, install via the [Shopify CLI](https://shopify.dev/docs/api/shopify-cli) against your dev/test store, or use the manual `<script>` install (see below). The Theme App Extension code lives in the InsiteChat repo at [`shopify-app/extensions/insitechat-widget/`](https://github.com/NitishSamurai/InsiteChat.ai/tree/prod/shopify-app/extensions/insitechat-widget).
</Note>

## Installation (App Store — recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the InsiteChat app">
    From your Shopify admin, search for **InsiteChat AI Chatbot** in the Shopify App Store and click **Add app**. Approve the requested permissions (we ask for `write_themes` so we can ship the chatbot block; we don't read or modify your products, orders, or customers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your theme editor">
    After install, Shopify drops you into **Online Store** → **Themes** → **Customize** with the InsiteChat block ready to enable. The block is auto-added to your theme's body section on Online Store 2.0 themes (Dawn, Sense, Crave, Refresh, Prestige, Impulse, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your Chatbot ID">
    Find your Chatbot ID on **insitechat.ai** → your chatbot → **Embed** tab. Format is a UUID like `0a1b2c3d-4e5f-6789-abcd-ef0123456789`. Paste it into the Chatbot ID field of the InsiteChat block in the theme editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose visibility (optional)">
    Two checkboxes, both **on** by default:

    | Setting | What it does |
    |---|---|
    | Hide on Cart & Checkout pages | Removes the chatbot from `/cart`, `/checkout`, and the Gift Card page so it never blocks a purchase. Recommended on. |
    | Hide on the Password page | Removes the chatbot on the storefront password page used by pre-launch stores. |

    Color, position (bottom-left vs bottom-right), language, and greeting are managed from your InsiteChat dashboard, not in the theme editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and preview">
    Click **Save** in the theme editor, then **Preview** the storefront. You should see the InsiteChat bubble in the corner. Click it — the chat panel opens at 430 × 640 on desktop and fullscreen on mobile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Installation (manual `<script>` tag — for vintage themes)

If your theme isn't Online Store 2.0 (Debut, Brooklyn, Express, custom Liquid themes), the App Store install can't auto-add the block. Instead:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open theme.liquid">
    **Online Store** → **Themes** → next to your active theme, **Edit code** → open `layout/theme.liquid`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the script tag">
    Just before the closing `</body>` tag, paste:

    ```liquid
    <script src="https://insitechat.ai/widget.js" data-chatbot-id="YOUR_CHATBOT_ID" async></script>
    ```

    Replace `YOUR_CHATBOT_ID` with the UUID from your **Embed** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save `theme.liquid`. The widget is live on every page that includes the layout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Manual installs don't get the cart/checkout auto-hide behavior. If you need that on a vintage theme, wrap the `<script>` tag in a Liquid conditional: `{% unless template.name == 'cart' or template.name == 'checkout' %}…{% endunless %}`.
</Warning>

## What the chatbot can do on Shopify

The chatbot answers questions grounded in **the public content of your store** that you've added as a training source on InsiteChat. That includes:

- Product titles, descriptions, variants, and prices
- Collection pages
- Shipping, returns, refund, privacy, and terms pages
- Blog posts
- Any custom URL you point InsiteChat's crawler at, even outside Shopify

It does **not** currently:

- Look up live inventory or order status from the Shopify Admin API. The chatbot answers from indexed page content only. (Live inventory checks are on the public roadmap.)
- Modify cart contents, apply discount codes, or initiate checkouts. The widget is a pure assistant — it can recommend products with links, but never acts on the merchant's behalf.

## Uninstall

**Settings** → **Apps and sales channels** → **InsiteChat AI Chatbot** → **Uninstall**.

The widget is removed from your storefront immediately. Shopify fires our `app/uninstalled` webhook so we mark your shop as uninstalled in our database. After 48 hours, Shopify fires the `shop/redact` webhook and we hard-delete the shop record per Shopify's GDPR policy.

If you also want to delete your InsiteChat account, do that separately at insitechat.ai → Settings → Delete account.

## Permissions we request

The app requests **`write_themes`** only. That's the minimum scope Shopify accepts for an app that ships theme assets. We don't:

- Read or modify products, variants, prices, inventory, collections
- Read or modify orders, customers, transactions
- Read store-level analytics or reports
- Read marketing campaigns, discounts, or storefront passwords

Concretely: at install time we receive an offline access token but never call any other Admin API endpoint. The chatbot is purely client-side.

## Troubleshooting

**"The InsiteChat block isn't showing up after install."** Your theme might be Vintage rather than OS 2.0. Use the manual `<script>` tag method above. To confirm, **Online Store** → **Themes** — Vintage themes are flagged with a yellow "Vintage" badge.

**"The widget appears on cart/checkout even though I enabled auto-hide."** Save the theme after enabling the toggle, and re-publish if the change was on a draft. Hard-refresh the storefront to bypass Shopify's CDN cache (a few minutes).

**"I changed the chatbot's color in InsiteChat but the Shopify widget didn't update."** Color, position, and other branding are pulled by `widget.js` at chat-open time. Visitors who already had the chat open on your store before the change will still see the old color until they reload. New visitors see the new color immediately.

**"Multi-store / multi-locale setup."** Install the app on each store. Use a separate InsiteChat chatbot ID per storefront so each store is trained on its own catalog. The widget itself supports per-store branding, language (English / Hindi / Hinglish out of the box), and RTL layouts.
