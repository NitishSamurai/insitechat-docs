## Overview

The InsiteChat Shopify app adds an AI chatbot to your Shopify store using a Theme App Extension. Visitors can ask product questions, get support, and submit leads — all powered by your trained knowledge base.

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install from Shopify App Store">
    Search for **InsiteChat** in the Shopify App Store, or install directly from your InsiteChat dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Chatbot ID">
    After installation, go to **Online Store** → **Themes** → **Customize**. Find the InsiteChat app block and enter your Chatbot ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Position">
    Choose where the chatbot appears: **Bottom Right** or **Bottom Left**. Save and publish your theme.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

- **Theme App Extension** — installs cleanly via Shopify's theme editor
- **No code changes** — no Liquid template editing required
- **Position control** — bottom-left or bottom-right
- **Works on all pages** — product pages, collections, cart, homepage
- **Shopify theme editor** — configure directly in your theme customizer
- **Mobile responsive** — works on all devices

## Finding Your Chatbot ID

1. Log in to [insitechat.ai](https://insitechat.ai)
2. Go to your chatbot → **Embed** tab
3. Copy the Chatbot ID

## Best Practices for Shopify

- **Train on product pages** — add your Shopify store URL as a source to train on all product descriptions
- **Add FAQ content** — upload your FAQ or return policy as a text source
- **Use Q&A pairs** — add common product questions with precise answers
- **Enable lead capture** — collect visitor emails for abandoned cart follow-ups

## Uninstalling

Go to **Settings** → **Apps** in your Shopify admin and uninstall InsiteChat. The chatbot widget is removed immediately.
