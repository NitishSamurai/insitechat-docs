# Quick Prompts

Quick prompts are pre-set conversation starters that appear as clickable buttons inside the chat widget. They help visitors get started instantly without having to type a question, and they guide conversations toward the topics you want to highlight.

## Why Use Quick Prompts

- **Reduce friction** — Visitors can start a conversation with a single click.
- **Guide conversations** — Steer users toward common questions or high-value topics.
- **Improve engagement** — Widgets with quick prompts see higher interaction rates.
- **Showcase capabilities** — Show visitors what your chatbot can help with.

## Set Up Quick Prompts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in to InsiteChat and select the chatbot you want to configure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Appearance settings">
    Click the **Appearance** tab in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Quick Prompts section">
    Scroll down to the **Quick Prompts** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your prompts">
    Click **Add prompt** and type the text you want to display on the button. Add up to 4 quick prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder if needed">
    Drag and drop to reorder the prompts. The first prompt appears in the most prominent position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click **Save**. Quick prompts appear immediately in your chat widget on the next page load.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
Keep quick prompts short and action-oriented. Phrases like "What's your return policy?" or "Show me pricing" work better than long, formal questions. Aim for under 40 characters per prompt.
</Tip>

<Info>
Quick prompts appear only on the initial screen of the chat widget, before the visitor has sent their first message. Once a conversation is active, the prompts are hidden.
</Info>

## Limits

- **Maximum prompts**: 4 per chatbot
- **Character limit**: 80 characters per prompt
- **Availability**: All plans (Free, Starter, Growth, Scale)
