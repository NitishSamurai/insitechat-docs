# Healthcare

Healthcare providers face repetitive inquiries about operational details every day. InsiteChat automates responses to routine questions, freeing staff to focus on direct patient care.

## Problems InsiteChat Solves for Healthcare Providers

- **Front desk staff overwhelmed** with routine calls about hours, services, and appointment availability.
- **Patients can't find information** on your practice website — they call or email instead.
- **After-hours inquiries go unanswered**, potentially driving patients to other providers.
- **Staff burnout** from answering identical administrative questions repeatedly.

## What to Train Your Chatbot On

InsiteChat works best when trained on the content patients ask about most:

- **Services and specialties** — what your practice offers and who it serves
- **Appointment scheduling** — how to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments
- **Insurance and billing** — accepted insurance plans, billing procedures, and payment options
- **Office locations, hours, and directions** — practical details for visiting your practice
- **Pre-appointment preparation** — what patients need to do or bring before their visit

## How to Set Up InsiteChat for Your Practice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your core documents">
    Go to **Sources** in your dashboard. Upload your services guide, patient FAQs, and insurance information as PDFs. Or use the website crawler to index your practice website automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add custom Q&A for high-volume questions">
    Open **Custom Q&A** and create entries for the questions your front desk answers most — appointment booking, insurance acceptance, office hours, and preparation instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Embed on your homepage and contact pages">
    Copy the embed snippet from **Deployment** and add it to the pages where patients look for help — typically your homepage, services page, and contact page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable lead capture">
    Turn on **Lead Capture** to collect patient contact information from visitors who interact with the chatbot, so your team can follow up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
InsiteChat is for informational support only. It should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
</Warning>

## What to Expect

- **Decreased call volume** for routine inquiries — your front desk handles fewer repetitive calls
- **24/7 patient access to information** — patients get answers outside office hours and on weekends
- **Staff focus on patient care** — administrative staff spend less time on the phone and more time supporting patients directly
