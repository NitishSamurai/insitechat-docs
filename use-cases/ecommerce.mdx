# E-commerce

InsiteChat helps online retailers provide instant product answers, handle routine support automatically, and gather leads around the clock. Train it on your store catalog and policies, embed it on your storefront, and let it work continuously.

## Problems InsiteChat Solves for E-commerce

- **Customers abandon purchases** because product questions go unanswered.
- **Support teams get overwhelmed** during peak sales periods.
- **Repetitive inquiries** consume significant staff resources.
- **Sales opportunities are lost** outside business hours.

## What to Train Your Chatbot On

Focus your chatbot's knowledge base on the content your customers ask about most:

- Product specifications and availability
- Shipping policies and timelines
- Return and refund procedures
- Size guides and compatibility information
- Frequently asked questions

<Tip>
If you have a size guide or compatibility chart as a PDF, upload it directly. InsiteChat reads structured documents well.
</Tip>

## How to Set Up InsiteChat for Your Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your product catalog">
    Go to **Sources** in your dashboard. Upload your catalog or use the website crawler to index your product pages automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add return and shipping policies">
    Add your return policy, shipping policy, and other store policies as separate knowledge sources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable lead capture">
    Turn on lead capture in your chatbot settings to collect visitor contact information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create custom Q&A pairs">
    Add custom Q&A entries for your highest-volume questions — like "What's your return policy?" or "How long does shipping take?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy the widget">
    Copy the embed snippet and paste it before the closing `</body>` tag on your storefront.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to Expect

- **Up to 70% fewer repetitive support tickets** — common questions handled automatically
- **24/7 lead collection** — capture visitor information even outside business hours
- **Reduced cart abandonment** — faster answers give customers the confidence to complete purchases
