# Quick Start

Get your AI chatbot up and running in six simple steps.

## 1. Create Your Account

Register at [insitechat.ai/register](https://insitechat.ai/register). Your account is activated immediately — no waiting for approval.

## 2. Create a Chatbot

From your dashboard, click **New chatbot** and give it a name. This name appears in the chat widget your visitors see.

## 3. Add a Knowledge Source

Train your chatbot by providing content it can learn from:

- **Website URL** — InsiteChat auto-crawls your site and indexes every accessible page.
- **File upload** — Upload a PDF, Word document, PowerPoint, plain text, or CSV file.

## 4. Customize the Appearance

Set the chatbot's display name, welcome message, and brand colors so it looks right at home on your site.

## 5. Embed on Your Website

Copy the JavaScript snippet from your dashboard and paste it into your website's HTML, just before the closing `</body>` tag.

## 6. Test and Refine

Open your site, ask the chatbot a few questions, and review its answers. If something isn't quite right, add more content or create custom Q&A pairs.

<Tip>
Custom Q&A pairs take priority over auto-extracted content, so the answers are always exactly what you intend.
</Tip>
