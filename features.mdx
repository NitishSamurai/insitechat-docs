## Human Support & Escalation

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reply from the Dashboard" icon="reply" href="/deployment/human-support#reply-from-the-dashboard">
    Take over an escalated conversation and reply directly to the visitor — works for **web widget, WhatsApp, and Telegram**. The AI auto-mutes for 24h while a human is engaged (rolling, refreshes on every reply).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Intent Detection" icon="sparkles" href="/deployment/human-support#smart-intent-detection">
    Detect "I need a real person" even when the visitor doesn't say the magic words. Keyword pre-filter + Gemini-powered LLM classifier with non-ASCII bypass for any language.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Inline Escalation CTAs" icon="life-ring" href="/deployment/human-support">
    After every bot reply, show a *positive feedback* button and a *connect to an agent* button — both labels and the post-escalation confirmation are fully customizable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Recipient Email Alerts" icon="envelope" href="/deployment/human-support">
    Send escalation emails to up to 10 addresses on top of the chatbot owner. Aliases, team buckets, on-call rotations all work.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Human Support Dashboard" icon="headset" href="/deployment/human-support">
    Dedicated triage view — filter by Open / Resolved, channel badge per row, read full transcripts, mark resolved with one click, deep-link from email.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Escalation Analytics" icon="chart-simple" href="/deployment/human-support">
    Total escalations, escalation rate, resolution rate, unresolved count, and average hours to resolve over the last 30 days.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Replace Suggestions Mode" icon="toggle-on" href="/deployment/human-support">
    Optionally hide smart follow-ups when the escalation CTA is shown — keeps the post-reply surface focused on a single decision.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unified Escalation Webhook" icon="bolt" href="/deployment/human-support#unified-webhook-payload">
    One `conversation.escalated` event for every channel with a `channel` field (`web` / `whatsapp` / `telegram`) and a `trigger` field — route to Slack, ticketing, or PagerDuty by source.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
