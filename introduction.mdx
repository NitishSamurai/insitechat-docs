InsiteChat is an AI-powered chatbot platform that helps you automate customer support, capture leads, and engage visitors 24/7. Train your chatbot on your website content, documents, and knowledge base to provide instant, accurate responses to customer questions.

## What InsiteChat Does

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Instant Answers" icon="message-bot">
    Answers customer questions instantly using your website, documentation, and knowledge base.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lead Capture" icon="address-book">
    Captures and qualifies leads with customizable forms embedded in the chat widget.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Human Handoff" icon="user-headset">
    Seamlessly escalates complex conversations to your team via email with full transcript.
  </Card>
  <Card title="13+ Integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect with Slack, Zapier, Telegram, HubSpot, Crisp, Freshchat, Zendesk, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto-Sync" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Automatically re-crawls your website on a schedule to keep answers up to date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Multi-Channel" icon="globe">
    Deploy on your website, WordPress, Shopify, Telegram, or connect to live chat platforms.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Conversations">
    InsiteChat uses advanced AI with hybrid search (semantic + keyword) and Reciprocal Rank Fusion to find the most relevant context from your content. Custom Q&A pairs take priority for human-verified answers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multi-Channel Deployment">
    Deploy your chatbot as a website widget, WordPress plugin, Shopify app, or Telegram bot. Each channel uses the same trained knowledge base and enforces the same billing limits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Rich Data Sources">
    Train on websites (automatic crawling), file uploads (PDF, Word, PowerPoint, CSV), Google Drive, Notion, Dropbox, and YouTube transcripts. All sources stay synced with auto-refresh.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Live Chat Integrations">
    Add AI auto-reply to your existing live chat — Crisp, Freshchat, or Zendesk. The AI handles first-line support and human agents can take over anytime.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Automation & CRM">
    Send events to Zapier (6,000+ apps), get Slack notifications for leads and escalations, and auto-sync leads to HubSpot CRM. All via webhooks with HMAC-SHA256 signing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Customization">
    Customize brand colors, display name, greeting message, input placeholder, widget position, and quick prompts. Remove InsiteChat branding on paid plans.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Train">
    Add your content — crawl your website, upload documents, or connect Google Drive, Notion, and Dropbox. InsiteChat chunks, embeds, and indexes everything automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize">
    Set your brand colors, greeting message, system prompt, and quick prompts. Configure lead capture forms and escalation settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Add a single line of code to your website, install the WordPress plugin, or connect to Telegram, Crisp, Freshchat, or Zendesk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor">
    Review conversations, track analytics, capture leads, and get real-time notifications via Slack or Zapier.
  </Step>
</Steps>
